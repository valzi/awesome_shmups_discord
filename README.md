## Harrier-likes

Space Harrier is a game about dodging scenery, shooting things quickly before they're gone, dodging aimed bullets, and automatically moving straight forward in a 3d world. Controls just reposition the player.

### Good Harrier-likes

- Space Harrier (3ds or revenge sega ages port recommended) 

- Space Harrier 2 (mega drive mini preferred) 

- Planet Harrier

- Space Fantasy Zone

- Panorama Cotton

- Burning Force

- Night Striker (recent m2 port is good. Saturn port has an arrange mode) 

- Galaxy Force 2 (ps2 recommended) 

- Air Twister

- Mission Metal Sight

- Neural Gear

### Honorable mentions (Good almost-Harriers)

- G-loc

- After Burners

- Panzer Dragoon

- Star Fox
  
- Star Fox 64
  
### Intentionally not included

- Rainbow Cotton (they say it's bad)

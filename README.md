## Harrier-likes

Space Harrier is a game about

- Dodging scenery

- Shooting things quick before they're not there

- Dodging aimed bullets

- Constantly moving straight forward

- Controls just reposition character
  
  ### Good Harrier-likes

- Space Harrier (3ds or revenge sega ages port recommended) 

- Space Harrier 2 (mega drive mini preferred) 

- Planet Harrier

- Space Fantasy Zone

- Panorama Cotton

- Burning Force

- Night Striker (recent m2 port is good. Saturn port has an arrange mode) 

- Galaxy Force 2 (ps2 recommended) 

- Air Twister

- Mission Metal Sight

- Neural Gear
  
  ### Honorable mentions (Good almost-Harriers)

- G-loc

- After Burners

- Panzer Dragoon

- Star Fox
  
  Star Fox 64
  
  ### Intentionally not included

- Rainbow Cotton (they say it's bad)
